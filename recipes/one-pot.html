One Pot Recipe

Description

Crispy Chicken thighs that look like it was cooked in the rice and seasoned well.

Ingrediants

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 6(4 ounce) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of kosher salt, divided
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked long grain rice
  • 3 cups of chicken stock,divided, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped flat-leaf parsely
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges
